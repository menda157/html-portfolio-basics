The best movies according to David

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Black bag

“Black Bag,” is an espionage thriller that feels like an update to Cold War-era stories like “Bridge of Spies” and “The Hunt For Red October”

Dead mail

“Dead Mail” begins as the story of Jasper (Tomas Boykin), a dead letter investigator in small-town Illinois who finds a mysterious, bloodied scrap of cardboard amid his usual pile of high-value items with incomplete addresses.

The Shrouds

The movie begins with a husband’s nightmare of his late wife’s naked body decomposing in a grave and gets grimmer and stranger from there.
